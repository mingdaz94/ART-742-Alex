The Stair Times

P1: Material Gathering

[Idea 1: Stair]

Stair makes it possible for people to use the space vertically. It's a spatial vector re-directing circulation in a 3d way. Composed by end to end modules, tread and rise, stair provides us a model to understand a space.

Method: 1.Collecting various interesting stairs online. 2. Collecting stair images/data from architecture students from their home/classrooms/projects.

Entries: as many as possible.

Time Period: through out the history.

[Idea 2: Facemask]

Facemask becomes our new identity during pandemic.

Method: Collecting images from Amazon/Walmart/...

Entries: as many as possible.

Time: When the first Covid case was found.

[Idea 3: Receipt]

Receipt documents our preference and routine, its a unique diary of our life.

Method: Collecting receipt from markets and classmates.

Entries: as many as possible.

Time: NA.
