The Stair Times

Stair as Urban Furniture

If a city is a building, stairs/stoops are furniture inside. As furniture in your home, which you can use in anyways you want, Stair is not only a part of circulation in a building but also a similar social generator to a city.

Stairs are different, however, they all composed of treads and risers, which provide a universal special container hosting different activities. Every day, people are generating stand, sit, listen, touch, exercise, play music, etc. on the stairs. By doing these, people keep leaving memories, thoughts, experiences, and anything on these elements.

This project intends to create a repository for collecting the locations in different cities, and their particular stories about these stairs.

---

How to Contribute?

If you are interested in contributing to this map, please send me one/two sentence(s) with the location of this stair! For the location, you can either send me the longitude and latitude data or just the street address.

Please just make sure, "stair" the word, appears in your sentences!
